Thesis Progress - Study Complete

The data collection portion of the research has been completed. In the end, data from seven participants was collected. One participant was forced to leave the study before completion due to personal reasons. Two other participants were generating poor data due to issues with sensor placement ( cranial size was not taken into account when selecting participants ) and a difficulty descrepency with respect to the tasks. Performance summaries and NASA TLX records have been created for each participant. While statistics have not been calculated yet, the trends seen by eye follow what was expected. Currently work is being done on scripts for the data processing, leveraging work done in my previous research with similarly formatted data. While slightly behind schedule as of now, hopefully that time can be made up within the next few days.
